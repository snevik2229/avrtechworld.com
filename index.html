Ethical hacking involves legally probing systems to identify and address security vulnerabilities before malicious hackers can exploit them. By simulating cyberattacks, ethical hackers help organizations strengthen their defenses and protect sensitive data, all while adhering to legal and professional standards.

Ethical hacking plays a crucial role in modern cybersecurity by proactively uncovering weaknesses in systems, networks, and applications. Certified professionals, known as ethical hackers, use the same techniques as malicious hackers but with permission and a focus on enhancing security. Their work helps organizations fortify their defenses, ensure compliance with regulations, and ultimately safeguard valuable data against real threats. By bridging the gap between security risks and protective measures, ethical hackers contribute to a safer digital landscape
